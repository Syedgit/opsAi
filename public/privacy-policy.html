Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

opsAi ("we", "our", or "us") operates a WhatsApp-based operations management system for convenience stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • WhatsApp Messages: Text messages and images you send via WhatsApp
  • Phone Numbers: Your WhatsApp phone number for account identification
  • Store Information: Store IDs and operational data you provide
  • Usage Data: Messages processed, timestamps, and system logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and classify your operational messages
  • Extract structured data from your messages
  • Write data to your Google Sheets
  • Send confirmation messages via WhatsApp
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) - encrypted at rest
  • Images: AWS S3 - secure cloud storage
  • Sheets: Google Sheets - your own Google account

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including: encryption, secure API connections, and access controls.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp Business API: For messaging functionality
  • Google Cloud Vision: For OCR (image text extraction)
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered data extraction
  • Google Sheets API: For data storage

These services have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data processing

Email: support@opsai.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
